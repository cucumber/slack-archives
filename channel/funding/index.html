I have some ideas and proposals for how we could help to fund Cucumber. Some are carrots, some are sticks:

  1. Carrot: A campaign to raise awareness of the projects's vulnerable state, and asking for sponsorship. We have a newsletter mailing list of around 5k emails that we can do this campaign with, plus we can use social media.

This seems unfair and worth proactively fixing.

One proposal would be to raise a PR for each of those repos that overrides the inherited funding settings, thus hiding the sponsorship suggestions. Said maintainers could then choose what they’re comfortable with having on the repo they look after.
